Quick reality check
  • Supported: macOS, Linux.
  • Not supported in the current setup workflow: Windows, WSL.
  • Strava required for authorization and sync (Client ID/Secret, one-time).
  • First sync may hit Strava API rate limits; higher-volume histories may need 15-minute resume windows.

What happens next

  1. The coach guides environment setup and Strava authorization.
  2. The coach syncs history and explains your baseline context.
  3. The coach builds a goal-based plan (v0 is running-first) and adapts it using your total training load.

Privacy and data boundary

Project data stays in your local workspace. Strava is only used for authorization and activity sync.
