Getting Started

Your AI coach runs inside Claude Code or Codex and guides every step. You don’t need to know the terminal.

15–25 minutes Local data only Beginner friendly

Quick Start

  1. Open Claude Code or Codex.
  2. Open this project folder inside the app.
  3. Send any message like: Help me get started or I’m not technical, please guide me

If you are unsure how to open the folder, just say so. The coach will walk you through it.

What You Need

  • A Strava account (free is fine).
  • 15–25 minutes for first-time setup.
  • Internet access for Strava connection.

You will create a Strava app one time and copy two values. The coach will explain exactly how.

What the Coach Will Do

  • Check your setup and install anything missing.
  • Connect your Strava account.
  • Sync your recent activities.
  • Create your profile and goals.
  • Propose a training plan and adapt it weekly.

Strava Authorization (What to Expect)

You will be asked to log in to Strava and approve access. Strava will show a link that includes a short code. You paste that code back to the coach.

This is normal and safe. The code simply lets the coach read your activities.

Keep your Strava Client Secret private. Do not share it with anyone except the local coach.

Your Data Stays Local

Your data lives on your computer. The only external service used is Strava for syncing activities.

Troubleshooting

Tell the coach what you see. It will diagnose the problem and guide you through a fix.

“I don’t see my activities.”

Ask the coach to re-sync your recent activities.

“I get a Strava error.”

Ask the coach to walk you through the Strava connection again.

After Setup

Just chat with the coach. Example prompts:

  • “How was my week?”
  • “What’s my workout today?”
  • “Adjust next week.”
